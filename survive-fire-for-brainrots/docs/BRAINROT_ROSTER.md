# Brainrot Roster — Survive Fire for Brainrots

**21 total**: 20 collectibles (5 per level) + 1 bonus + 1 final boss.

Each brainrot has:
- **Lore vibe** (one-liner)
- **Visual** (silhouette-defining traits)
- **Pickup behavior** (how they sit/wander in the level)
- **Voice lines** (3–5 short clips)
- **Boss-fight summon ability** (cooldown, duration, effect)

Cooldowns are tuned for a 3-slot loadout. Damage values are placeholders (DV = damage value, normalized so the boss has 1000 HP at default).

---

## Level 1 — The Whirling Halls (Temple)

### 1. Bombombini Gusini
- **Lore**: An eternally angry goose strapped with cartoon bombs.
- **Visual**: White goose, bandolier of round black bombs, tiny goggles.
- **Pickup**: Stands honking near a pillar.
- **Voice**: "Bombombini! Gusini!" / "HONK!" / "Esplosione!"
- **Summon**: **Bomb Volley** — Lobs 5 bombs in an arc; CD 18s; duration 3s; AOE 4m, 60 DV each.

### 2. Brr Brr Patapim
- **Lore**: A tree-monkey who whispers wind incantations.
- **Visual**: Bark-skinned simian with leafy mane, huge yellow eyes.
- **Pickup**: Hangs upside-down from a beam.
- **Voice**: "Brr brr patapim..." / "Vento!" / "Patapim, patapam!"
- **Summon**: **Whisperwind** — Creates a slipstream behind the player; CD 25s; duration 8s; +50% sprint speed and dodge i-frames extended.

### 3. Trippi Troppi
- **Lore**: Half cat, half shrimp. Doesn't make sense, doesn't have to.
- **Visual**: Orange tabby head + striped shrimp body + tiny crustacean legs.
- **Pickup**: Skitters in a circle, easy to grab.
- **Voice**: "Trippi-troppi-trippi-troppi!" / "Mrrow-tch-tch-tch"
- **Summon**: **Skitter Swarm** — Spawns 6 tiny clones that home toward the boss; CD 22s; duration 6s; 30 DV per clone hit.

### 4. Boneca Ambalabu
- **Lore**: A cursed doll-frog-tire fused into one entity.
- **Visual**: Frog body with porcelain doll face on top, sitting in a black rubber tire.
- **Pickup**: Idle, blinks slowly. Disturbing.
- **Voice**: (long unsettling silence) / "Boneca... ambalabu..." / "Ribbit-tick"
- **Summon**: **Cursed Tire** — Hurls itself as a rolling tire that bounces; CD 20s; duration 4s; 80 DV on hit, knocks boss back 3m.

### 5. Lirili Larila *(boss-of-level)*
- **Lore**: A cactus-elephant who controls time with a pocket watch.
- **Visual**: Elephant body covered in cactus needles, holding a pocket watch in its trunk, sandals.
- **Pickup**: At the end of a longer chase corridor; runs from player and must be cornered.
- **Voice**: "Lirili larila!" / "Tic-toc, tic-toc..." / "Tempo!"
- **Summon**: **Time Stutter** — Slows boss by 60% for the duration; CD 45s; duration 5s; no direct damage but enables a free combo window.

## Level 2 — The Falling Sky (Wasteland)

### 6. Bombardiro Crocodilo
- **Lore**: A WWII-style bomber plane fused with a crocodile head.
- **Visual**: Crocodile head with airplane body and wings, dropping bombs from belly bay.
- **Pickup**: Circles a high spire — must be caught from above.
- **Voice**: "Bombardiro crocodilo!" / "VRRRRMMM!" / "Bomba via!"
- **Summon**: **Carpet Bomb** — Flies overhead and drops a line of 8 bombs; CD 35s; duration 4s; 50 DV each.

### 7. Cappuccino Assassino
- **Lore**: A coffee-cup ninja, deadly and over-caffeinated.
- **Visual**: Ceramic espresso cup with ninja headband, two katanas crossed on its back, swirling foam-eyes.
- **Pickup**: Hidden behind a destructible meteor shard.
- **Voice**: "Cappuccino assassino!" / "Whoosh!" / "Espresso!"
- **Summon**: **Shadow Strike** — Teleports onto boss and unleashes a 5-hit combo; CD 28s; duration 3s; 40 DV per hit.

### 8. Tung Tung Tung Sahur
- **Lore**: A wooden cudgel-creature awakened during Sahur (pre-dawn meal during Ramadan).
- **Visual**: Vertical wooden bat with single eye, holding a baseball bat in its tiny arms.
- **Pickup**: Stands rhythmically tapping the ground.
- **Voice**: "Tung tung tung tung tung Sahur!" / "Tok tok tok!"
- **Summon**: **Wakeup Slam** — Slams ground in front of player creating a 6m radius stun wave; CD 22s; duration instant; 70 DV + 2s stun.

### 9. Frigo Camelo
- **Lore**: A walking refrigerator on camel legs.
- **Visual**: White fridge with camel humps on top, four bowed camel legs, fridge door swings open to reveal an icy interior.
- **Pickup**: Walks slowly on a fixed patrol path along a high ridge.
- **Voice**: "Frigo camelo!" / "Brrrr..." / (door slams)
- **Summon**: **Cold Storage** — Opens its door, freezing boss in place; CD 50s; duration 4s; 0 DV but full immobilize.

### 10. Ballerina Cappuccina *(boss-of-level)*
- **Lore**: A graceful ballerina with a coffee cup for a head.
- **Visual**: Pink tutu, cappuccino-cup head with foam-art smile, en pointe.
- **Pickup**: Performs an endless pirouette; player must time their grab to her rhythm.
- **Voice**: "Ballerina cappuccina!" / "Una, due, tre!" / (delicate humming)
- **Summon**: **Grand Jeté** — Performs a sweeping dance that hits all enemies in a 10m arc; CD 30s; duration 4s; 120 DV total.

## Level 3 — The Bloop's Domain (Ocean)

### 11. Tralalero Tralala
- **Lore**: A three-legged shark wearing blue Nike sneakers, the patron saint of brainrot.
- **Visual**: Shark with three muscular legs, three blue Nikes, classic toothy grin.
- **Pickup**: Sprints across underwater coral, hard to catch.
- **Voice**: "Tralalero tralala!" / "Porco Dio!" (censored or replaced for child-friendliness) / "Sneaky-sneaky!"
- **Summon**: **Three-Legged Tackle** — Charges through boss; CD 24s; duration 2s; 100 DV; passes through.

### 12. Burbaloni Luliloli
- **Lore**: A coconut-shell capybara floating peacefully.
- **Visual**: Capybara curled inside half a coconut shell, like a tiny boat.
- **Pickup**: Floats lazily near the surface.
- **Voice**: "Burbaloni luliloli..." / (gentle squeak) / "Lo-li-lo-li!"
- **Summon**: **Splash Heal** — Splashes water on player, healing 2 HP; CD 60s; duration instant.

### 13. Orangutini Ananasini
- **Lore**: An orangutan whose entire body is a pineapple.
- **Visual**: Orangutan head and limbs sticking out of a pineapple body, tiny crown of leaves.
- **Pickup**: Hides in a pineapple grove (5 fakes, 1 real — must touch each).
- **Voice**: "Orangutini ananasini!" / "Ananas!" / (chimpanzee hoot)
- **Summon**: **Pineapple Toss** — Lobs spiked pineapples at boss; CD 18s; duration 5s; 35 DV per pineapple.

### 14. La Vacca Saturno Saturnita
- **Lore**: A celestial cow with rings of Saturn around her belly.
- **Visual**: Black-and-white cow with glowing planetary rings of dust orbiting her midsection.
- **Pickup**: Floats above the seafloor; player must build a "stack" of brainrots tall enough to reach (or use a coral platform).
- **Voice**: "La vacca saturno saturnita!" / "Mooooo..." / (cosmic hum)
- **Summon**: **Gravity Well** — Pulls boss to center of arena; CD 40s; duration 3s; 50 DV + reposition.

### 15. Chimpanzini Bananini *(boss-of-level)*
- **Lore**: A chimpanzee whose torso is a banana.
- **Visual**: Chimp head, banana body (peel partially open), monkey arms and legs.
- **Pickup**: Riding on the back of the Bloop during the chase sequence — grab during the chase finale.
- **Voice**: "Chimpanzini bananini!" / "OOH OOH AH AH!" / "Bananini!"
- **Summon**: **Banana Slip** — Throws a banana peel at boss feet; CD 30s; duration instant; 0 DV but 4s prone state (boss flat on its face).

## Level 4 — The Lava Crucible (Volcano)

### 16. Bambini Crustini
- **Lore**: A piece of toast with a baby's face.
- **Visual**: Square slice of golden-brown toast with cooing baby face, jam smeared like a beard.
- **Pickup**: On a wobbly platform; tilt sensitive.
- **Voice**: "Bambini crustini!" / "Goo goo!" / "Crunch crunch crunch"
- **Summon**: **Buttered Slide** — Throws toast on the ground creating a slick patch; CD 25s; duration 8s; boss slides into walls for 50 DV per wall hit.

### 17. Garamararmaduro
- **Lore**: An armadillo with armor of medieval steel.
- **Visual**: Armadillo curled in plate-mail shell, helmet on head.
- **Pickup**: Rolls between geysers in a fixed loop.
- **Voice**: "Garamararmaduro!" / "CLANG!" / "Onore!"
- **Summon**: **Iron Shell** — Curls in front of player as a damage shield; CD 35s; duration 6s; absorbs all damage.

### 18. Trulimero Trulicina
- **Lore**: A fish-cat-human chimera with four legs.
- **Visual**: Fish body, cat head, human legs (four of them).
- **Pickup**: Dashes across the lava on its four human legs (somehow doesn't burn).
- **Voice**: "Trulimero trulicina!" / "Squelch-meow!" / (giggle)
- **Summon**: **Confusion Dance** — Confuses boss, who attacks the wrong direction; CD 30s; duration 5s; 0 DV; boss attacks miss.

### 19. Glorbo Fruttodrillo
- **Lore**: A watermelon-crocodile hybrid.
- **Visual**: Watermelon body with crocodile head and tail emerging from it, seeds visible inside.
- **Pickup**: Lurks in a small lava pool (heat-resistant somehow), pops up periodically.
- **Voice**: "Glorbo fruttodrillo!" / "ROAR-glug" / (crunch)
- **Summon**: **Seed Spit** — Rapid-fires watermelon seeds at boss; CD 20s; duration 4s; 25 DV per seed, 12 seeds.

### 20. Lirili Larila Tralala *(boss-of-level — fire variant)*
- **Lore**: A flaming alternate version of Lirili Larila, fused with a Tralalero shark fin.
- **Visual**: Cactus-elephant with shark fin and a flaming pocket watch, blazing aura.
- **Pickup**: At the volcano summit; chase sequence with collapsing platforms.
- **Voice**: "Lirili larila tralala!" / "FUOCO!" / "Tic-toc-FIRE!"
- **Summon**: **Time Burn** — Slows boss AND inflicts burn DOT; CD 45s; duration 6s; 200 DV total + slow.

## Bonus — Unlocked After Level 4

### 21. Tracotucotulu Delapelapeladusduzdas
- **Lore**: The brainrot with the longest name in the universe. Appears only when all 20 are gathered.
- **Visual**: An impossible chimera — bits of every other brainrot stacked into a tall, swaying tower of fur, scales, fruit, machinery, and limbs. Crowned with a tiny coffee cup.
- **Unlock**: Cinematic at end of Level 4 — after returning the 20th brainrot, all 20 form into Tracotucotulu in a beam of light.
- **Voice**: A ~12-second voice line that recites its full name plus all 20 names of the others.
- **Summon (always in loadout, free slot)**: **Brainrot Cascade** — All 20 collected brainrots fly through the boss in sequence, dealing chained damage; CD 90s; duration 8s; 30 DV per brainrot = up to 600 DV total.

## Final Boss

### Georglini Dragonfruitini
- **Lore**: The ancient ruler of the brainrot dimension. Imprisoned all the brainrots and dares the player to challenge his throne.
- **Visual**: Massive (5m tall) magenta-pink dragon-fruit-dragon hybrid, scaled spiked exterior, glowing yellow seed-eyes, coffee-cup crown, six wings made of seed-pods.
- **Voice lines**: deep, distorted, faux-Italian villain — "Georglini! DRAGONFRUITINI!" / "Tu osi sfidarmi?!" / (laughs maniacally) / "Io sono il VERO brainrot!"

### Stats
| Phase | HP gate | Move set additions |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1000 → 660 | Ground slam, fireball, claw swipe |
| 2 | 660 → 330 | + Dive bomb, seed barrage |
| 3 | 330 → 0 | Enrage (all CDs halved); + Mini-Georglini spawns |

### Win
At HP 0, Georglini explodes into 21 sparkles, one for each brainrot. Cinematic plays. Player returns home through a portal.

## Voice Line Production Notes

- **Source style**: original brainrot meme audio uses Italian-accented AI TTS. We'll match this for in-game lines.
- **Tools**: ElevenLabs / Suno voice clones (with appropriate license), or Coqui TTS open-source.
- **Counts**: ~3–5 lines per brainrot × 21 = ~80–100 short voice clips. Mostly 0.5–2 sec each.
- **Localization**: English fallback subtitles for non-Italian-speaking players.

## Summon Ability Balance Targets

For a default 3-slot loadout (+ free Tracotucotulu in slot 4):

- **Sustained DPS** if all 4 are perfectly rotated: ~80–100 DV/sec
- **Boss HP**: 1000
- **Expected fight length**: 2–4 minutes
- **Player melee DPS** (basic attack): ~15 DV/sec
- → Fight is ~70% summons, ~30% melee — tune in playtesting.
